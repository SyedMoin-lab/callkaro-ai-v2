Live Demo sample recordings
===========================

Drop your MP3 files here with these exact names — the Live Demo section on the
home page plays them when you press "Play sample call" for each use case:

  receptionist.mp3
  appointment-setter.mp3
  lead-qualification.mp3
  customer-service.mp3
  debt-collection.mp3
  survey.mp3

They are served at /audio/<name>.mp3. To rename or add use cases, edit
src/app/(home)/_liveDemo/params.ts (the `audio` field on each use case).
