**What it does**, The agent answers in real time, with the quick, natural timing that keeps callers engaged instead of waiting through dead air.

**What it covers**, Sub-second response times, streaming speech recognition, and smooth turn-taking, even during peak volume. The experience stays fast whether you are running ten calls or ten thousand.

**How it works**, Speech is transcribed and understood as the caller speaks, so the agent can start forming its reply before they finish. Responses stream back instantly, which makes the conversation feel like talking to a person, not a system.

**Where it shines**, A direct-to-consumer brand used CallKaro AI to carry peak festive-season volume around the clock, resolving the large majority of calls end to end without adding a temporary team.
