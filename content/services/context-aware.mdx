**What it does**, The agent understands what each caller actually wants and responds in a way that fits your business, your policies, and your tone, not a one-size-fits-all script.

**What it covers**, Live intent and context tracking, custom logic for your specific workflows, grounding in your own knowledge base, and clear rules for when to hand a call to a person.

**How it works**, You give the agent your content and your rules once. On every call it reads intent, pulls the right facts, and follows your logic, before, during, or after the conversation, so responses stay accurate and on-brand.

**Where it shines**, A mid-size insurer used CallKaro AI to run monthly renewal calls that answered policy questions in context and scheduled callbacks when customers were busy. More policies renewed on time, every cycle.
