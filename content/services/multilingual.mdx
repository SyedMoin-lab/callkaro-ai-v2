**What it does**, Your AI voice agent greets every caller in the language they are most comfortable with, and adapts to regional accents so conversations feel natural from the first word.

**What it covers**, More than twenty languages out of the box, automatic detection of the caller's language, and accent-aware understanding so nobody has to repeat themselves. Scripts, tone, and pronunciation are localised for each market you operate in.

**How it works**, The agent detects language and intent in real time, then responds in a natural, human-sounding voice tuned for that language. You can set a default language per number or campaign, and let the agent switch on the fly when a caller prefers another.

**Where it shines**, An edtech platform used CallKaro AI to answer admissions enquiries around the clock in half a dozen languages. Every enquiry got an instant answer, and completed applications rose sharply.
