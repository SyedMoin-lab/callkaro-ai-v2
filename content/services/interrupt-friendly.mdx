**What it does**, Real conversations are messy. Callers cut in, change their minds, and ask something else halfway through. The agent takes it in stride, just like a good human rep.

**What it covers**, Smooth handling of interruptions, instant recovery of context when the topic shifts, and natural back-and-forth that never feels like a rigid phone tree.

**How it works**, The agent listens while it speaks, so a caller can interrupt at any point and be understood right away. It holds the thread of the conversation, so even after a detour it picks up exactly where things left off.

**Where it shines**, A regional power utility used CallKaro AI to absorb billing-cycle call spikes. Callers could jump straight to their question, and the agent resolved the large majority of calls without a human.
