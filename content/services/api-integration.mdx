**What it does**, CallKaro AI fits into the tools you already use. Your agent can read and write to your systems in real time, so calls trigger real actions instead of just capturing notes.

**What it covers**, Native sync with your CRM and help desk, custom functions the agent can call before, during, or after a conversation, post-call webhooks for every call, and clean REST and batch APIs.

**How it works**, Connect your systems once with developer-friendly APIs and custom functions. The agent fetches live data mid-call, updates records automatically, and fires a webhook with the recording, transcript, and structured outcomes the moment a call ends.

**Where it shines**, A consumer-lending firm wired CallKaro AI to its daily due-accounts file and CRM. Thousands of compliant reminder calls went out automatically, every conversation was logged, and on-time payments more than doubled.
