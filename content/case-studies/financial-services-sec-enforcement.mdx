**The Client**, A mid-size insurance provider with hundreds of thousands of policies coming up for renewal each month.

**The Challenge**, Renewal reminders depended on an over-stretched tele-calling team. Many customers were reached at bad moments, said "call me later," and were never called back, so policies quietly lapsed.

**What CallKaro AI Did**, The voice agent runs the monthly renewal list, explains the renewal clearly, and answers policy questions from the knowledge base. When a customer is busy, auto-callback captures the requested time and the agent calls back exactly then.

**The Results**, More policies now renew on time each cycle, and the retention team spends its energy on high-value conversations instead of chasing dials.
