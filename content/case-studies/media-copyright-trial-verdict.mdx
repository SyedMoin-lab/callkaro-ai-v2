**The Client**, An edtech platform enrolling students nationwide across dozens of courses.

**The Challenge**, Admissions enquiries came in at all hours and in many languages, far more than the counselling team could answer quickly. Slow responses meant interested students drifted away before applying.

**What CallKaro AI Did**, We built a multilingual voice agent on a knowledge base of course details, fees, and eligibility. It answers inbound enquiries instantly, qualifies interest, and books a live counsellor session through calendar-booking for serious applicants. Outbound follow-ups re-engage students who went quiet.

**The Results**, Every enquiry now gets an instant, accurate answer, and far more applications reach completion. Counsellors spend their time with the warmest, best-qualified prospects.
