**The Client**, A network of outpatient clinics across four cities, booking tens of thousands of appointments a month.

**The Challenge**, No-shows ran high and unpredictable. Front-desk staff called patients by hand to confirm visits, which was slow, inconsistent, and impossible to keep up with at scale.

**What CallKaro AI Did**, We connected a voice agent to the clinics' Cal.com scheduling through calendar-booking integration. It calls each patient ahead of time, confirms or reschedules in real time against live availability, and detects voicemail to leave a clear callback message.

**The Results**, No-shows dropped by more than a third within the first two months. Rescheduling now happens on the call itself, freeing front-desk teams and keeping clinicians' calendars full.
