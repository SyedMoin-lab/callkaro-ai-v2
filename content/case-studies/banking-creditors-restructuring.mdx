**The Client**, A consumer-lending business managing a large book of small-ticket loans across the country.

**The Challenge**, Reminder calls were expensive, inconsistent, and hard to audit. Agents could only reach a fraction of due accounts each day, and compliance worried about what was actually said on the phone.

**What CallKaro AI Did**, We set up batch calling from the firm's daily due-accounts file. The voice agent places thousands of reminder calls at once, follows an approved compliant script, captures intent, and books a callback when a customer asks for one. Every call is transcribed for review.

**The Results**, Right-party contact rates rose sharply and on-time payments more than doubled, while a complete, auditable record of every conversation gave compliance full visibility.
