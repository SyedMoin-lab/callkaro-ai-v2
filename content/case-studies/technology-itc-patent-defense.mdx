**The Client**, A fast-growing direct-to-consumer brand whose support volume triples during festive sales.

**The Challenge**, Seasonal spikes forced expensive hiring and training cycles for temporary agents, and customers still waited on hold for simple questions like "where's my order?"

**What CallKaro AI Did**, We gave the agent a knowledge base built from the brand's help centre and order system, so it answers tracking, returns, and policy questions with live data. When a customer needs a human, it transfers the call with the full conversation so far.

**The Results**, The agent now resolves the large majority of calls end to end, at any hour, and holds steady through peak volume. Human agents handle only genuine exceptions, and the brand skipped its seasonal hiring spree entirely.
