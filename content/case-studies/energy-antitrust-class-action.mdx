**The Client**, A regional power utility serving several million households, with call volumes that spike hard at every billing cycle and during outages.

**The Challenge**, Seasonal surges left customers waiting on hold for up to forty minutes, and a growing backlog of missed calls that agents chased for days. Most of the volume was routine: balance checks, due dates, outage status, and payment reminders.

**What CallKaro AI Did**, We deployed a voice agent connected to the utility's billing and outage systems through custom functions, so it reads real account data live on the call. It answers in the customer's language, resolves routine requests end to end, and transfers anything complex to a human with full context.

**The Results**, Within six weeks the callback backlog reached zero. The agent now resolves the large majority of inbound calls without a human, and post-call analysis flags emerging outage clusters before they overwhelm the contact centre.
